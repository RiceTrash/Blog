01 Day One

Flight and First Explorations

From Zamboanga to Manila, discovering historic Intramuros and the commercial heart of Asia

We kicked off our tour by arriving at Zamboanga Airport at 8:25 AM, heading straight to NAIA Terminal 3 in Manila, where we landed around 2 PM. After a short break, we boarded the tour bus and made our first stop ‚Äî the historic Intramuros.

Intramuros offered a glimpse into Spanish colonial architecture, later repurposed by American forces during their occupation. Some areas even served as Japanese torture chambers during World War II, making the visit both insightful and sobering.

Next, we headed to SM Mall of Asia (MOA), one of the largest malls in Asia. The place isn't just a shopping hub‚Äîit attracts large-scale investments and global brands, reflecting the commercial strength of the Philippines.
